HOW TO PLAY THIS GAME:

  1. Click on the blue guy (wooper)
  2. Get money and spend it on hats
  3. Buy mushrooms for the wooper to increase money gain from clicking
  4. Hope you enjoy this game!

Note: This is very much a game in progress. At the moment, it isn't very playable, but it's coming along.
